Lower perplexity scores indicate more fluent, natural language.

About: This tool uses perplexity (PPL) to measure how "surprised" a language model is by text. Lower perplexity scores suggest more natural, human-like writing.

Try it with: News articles (should score low), random word jumbles (should score high), or AI-generated text (often scores somewhere in between).

Inspired by the perplexity-based scoring from the LongCodeZip paper (arXiv:2510.00446).
